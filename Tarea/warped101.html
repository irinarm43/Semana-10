YOUR GUIDE TO ALL THINGS WARPED


First time at Warped or just need a refresher on what to expect at Warped? No worries. This handy guide will help you in planning your day at Warped and answer questions you might have about some things that make our tour unique. Here's your guide to and how to get through Warped!Remember to have fun, be safe and rock out!!

Before the Show

IS THERE AN AGE LIMIT FOR WARPED?


Nope - Warped Tour is an all ages show!
NOTE: we strongly suggest children under 8 years of age do not attend due to the heat and loud environment

WHERE DO I GET TICKETS?


SUPER FAN BUNDLES: Right now, there are limited edition ticket bundles available; these are superfan packages that will likely sell out soon.Tickets
ONLINE: general admission tickets to all shows are available on our website: Tickets
VARIOUS LOCAL OUTLETS: Certain shows sell tickets via local record shops, venue box office, etc. For full details head to your individual show page: Tickets

WHEN ARE MY TICKETS GOING TO BE MAILED?


Mail to home tickets are often mailed out 3 to 6 weeks before your tour stop, but they can be delivered up to one week before your show.
Shipping times vary by market; for specific questions on your order, contact the customer service department for your show’s ticketing company. Be sure to have your order number and details ahead of your email/call.

WHEN ARE MY WARPED 3D SOUVENIR OR COLLECTOR TICKETS GOING TO BE MAILED?


The Warped Souvenir and Collector tickets are often mailed out 3 to 6 weeks before your tour stop, but they can be delivered up to one week before your show.
Shipping times vary per show; for specific questions on your order, contact the customer service department for your show’s ticketing company. Be sure to have your order number and details ahead of your email/call.

At the Show


IS THE EVENT RAIN OR SHINE?


Yes - We will stop the show and have the fans seek shelter if there is extreme weather, but the Vans Warped Tour will happen rain or shine!

DROP OFF AREA


Most venues have a designated area in front of the venue for drop offs and pick-ups. Make sure to coordinate a pick up location with your friends and parents before going into the event!

DO NOT BUY TICKETS IN THE PARKING LOT


These are often counterfeit tickets and we are not responsible for fake tickets. You'll lose your money and won't be able to enter the show.
Instead, just head over to the venue box office by the main gates and buy a ticket there!

WHERE ARE THE BAND SIGNINGS?


Visit the sponsor, non-profit, and band tents to write down times for signings, acoustic performances and more! Sponsors and bands participating will have this information posted outside of their tents each day.
